As per requirements of this semester I had to apply for an internship that would last at least 3 weeks. 

I was terrified, not just because it's a new place, new people, and just overall too many things could go wrong. But mainly because I do computational chemistry. I create simulation, and I'm more a data scientist that a girly in a laboratory. So, the idea of being in a professional laboratory just made me stay awake at night. 

But here I am at a last week of the internship, and I can assure you that it is amazing. 

I got into a firm Membrain that creates all sorts of membranes for different usages.
