I was recently inspired by this [blog post](https://mnemonics.neocities.org/#originsofalchemy) made by Mnemonics. In this post they talk about how great philosophers of Ancient Greece were among first who proposed the theoretical background of the alchemical theory of matter. This beautiful summary gave me an idea to dive deeper into the origins of ALCHEMY.

This fact is already stated in the [About me](https://alchemistsnotes.com/about-me/) section of this website, but I would like to reiterate about the reasons. I am a chemistry girl, chemistry runs in my blood and I breathe chemistry. Chemistry is quite literally my whole life. I set my life for academic journey, and hopefully it will lead to me becoming a professor or just a teacher in a college. That is my dream. But back to the blog itself.
