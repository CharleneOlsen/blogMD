your presence
made me cry that day

I felt safe
yet I knew 
I couldn’t stay there

I'm always so afraid of warmth
yet I seek it 
everywhere

I forget how unfamiliar  
warmth has become

like a shot of whiskey 
you poured me that day
felt so empty
with its artificial warmth

yet it stirred all those feeling
that laid on the bottom of the dead lake

now its all dust
and I don't see clearly
what I was reaching for
