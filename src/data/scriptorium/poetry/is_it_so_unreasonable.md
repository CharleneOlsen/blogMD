is it so unreasonable
to be excited over small things
to be mesmerized over the simplest laws of nature

is it so horrible
to have deep sense of empathy 
for all beings in this world

is it so unreasonable
to be enchanted by falling leaves
by pouring rain that I feel on my lips

is it so bad
to be myself
to love myself
to not feel disappointed by myself 
every...
single...
day...

is it so unreasonable
to feel
to just want to live
to share
to give

is it so weird
to walk in the mist
and talk to myself
about everything
